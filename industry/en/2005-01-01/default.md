# PC EMS/ODM: Electronics Manufacturing Services

By 2005, the line between simple contract manufacturing and sophisticated design had blurred, with Taiwan leading the world in Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) and Original Design Manufacturing (ODM) for the PC industry. Taiwanese firms were now responsible for the assembly and testing of over 80% of the world's PCs.

The industry was dominated by giants like Hon Hai Precision Industry (Foxconn), Quanta, Compal, and Pegatron. These companies developed massive economies of scale and highly optimized supply chains that could churn out millions of devices with high precision and low cost. Foxconn, in particular, became a global icon of this manufacturing prowess.

This era marked Taiwan's transition into an indispensable "One-Stop Shop" for global tech giants. From component sourcing to final assembly and global logistics, Taiwanese EMS/ODM providers offered a level of integration that allowed the digital revolution to scale globally at an unprecedented pace.
