# IC Packaging Leadership and High-end Bicycles

In 2010, Taiwan demonstrated its industrial versatility by achieving global leadership in two vastly different yet highly sophisticated sectors: IC Packaging and Testing (OSAT) and high-end finished bicycles.

In the semiconductor world, Taiwan's ASE Group (Advanced Semiconductor Engineering) became the world's largest provider of independent semiconductor packaging and testing services. As chips became smaller and more complex, Taiwan's leadership in backend services became as critical as chip fabrication itself, ensuring the reliability of the global tech ecosystem.

Simultaneously, Taiwan's bicycle industry reached a new height. Giant Manufacturing became the world's largest bicycle company by revenue, proving that a Taiwanese brand could lead the world in a lifestyle and sports category. This dual success showcased Taiwan's unique ability to excel in both invisible high-tech infrastructure and visible consumer brand leadership, driven by a relentless focus on quality and innovation.
