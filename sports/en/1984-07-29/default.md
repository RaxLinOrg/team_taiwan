# The Catalyst for Weightlifting Glory

On July 29, 1984, Tsai Wen-yee won a bronze medal in the men's 60kg category at the Los Angeles Olympics. This achievement was the first Olympic medal for "Chinese Taipei" and served as a major catalyst for the development of Taiwan's weightlifting program, particularly paving the way for the island's future world-class dominance in women's weightlifting.
