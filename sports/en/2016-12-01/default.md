# Tai Tzu-ying World No. 1

In December 2016, badminton superstar Tai Tzu-ying reached the world number one ranking in women's singles for the first time. She went on to hold the top spot for a record-breaking 214 weeks.

Known for her deceptive shots and incredible agility, Tai is widely considered one of the greatest female badminton players of all time.
